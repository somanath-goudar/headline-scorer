Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

Short version: We collect no personal data. Your headlines are analysed entirely in your browser and never sent to any server.

What Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect the headlines or email subjects you analyse.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information.
  • We do not use tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or behavioural analytics.
  • No account registration is required.

How the Tool Works

The Headline Analyzer runs entirely inside your browser. All scoring, power word detection, suggestion generation, and alternative headline creation happen locally using JavaScript embedded in the page. No headline text is transmitted to any server.

History Feature & Local Storage

The tool saves your last 10 headlines to your browser's localStorage for convenient access. This data is stored only on your own device and is never sent to us or any third party. You can clear this history at any time using the "Clear all" button in the tool.

Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser type, page requested, timestamp) as part of normal web hosting operations. See Vercel's Privacy Policy for details.

If we add advertising (e.g. Google AdSense) in future, this policy will be updated before any ads are displayed.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the tool after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
